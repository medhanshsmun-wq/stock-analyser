## Nexus AI Stock Analyzer

Nexus AI Stock Analyzer is an AI-powered stock research platform built to deliver fast, structured, and intelligent market insights through a conversational interface. Users can simply ask the AI chatbot to analyze any stock, and the system generates a comprehensive breakdown combining data, sentiment, and predictive signals.

At its core, the platform features an Analysis Hub that aggregates up to two years of historical market data, enabling users to evaluate price trends, volatility, and overall performance in a clean, intuitive format. The centerpiece of the platform is the Nexus AI Analyzer, which assigns each stock a proprietary score out of 100 based on multiple parameters including technical structure, momentum, and sentiment.

The AI architecture is designed as a specialized financial intelligence system. It operates as a wrapper over Gemini AI, where the penultimate layer processes raw financial and contextual data, and a refinement layer transforms this output into a domain-specific financial expert. This layered approach ensures that responses are not only accurate but also tailored for investment decision-making.

To provide broader perspective, the platform compares its AI-generated ratings with Wall Street analyst consensus, helping users identify alignment or divergence between institutional opinions and AI-driven insights. It also classifies stocks into bullish or bearish outlooks, highlights potential opportunities, and flags key risks.

### Key Features

* Conversational AI chatbot for stock analysis
* Historical data insights (up to 2 years)
* Nexus AI scoring system (/100 rating)
* Gemini-based architecture with financial refinement layer
* Wall Street analyst comparison
* Bullish/Bearish sentiment analysis
* Opportunity and risk identification

Nexus AI Stock Analyzer bridges the gap between raw financial data and actionable intelligence, empowering users with institutional-grade analysis in a streamlined, user-friendly experience.
